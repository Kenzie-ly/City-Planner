1. Heavy industrial facilities must not be placed within residential zones.
2. Monumental buildings must not be placed within green zones.
3. Public spaces must be limited within green zones; maximum one per zone.
4. Industrial buildings must be at least 5 km from schools and hospitals.
5. High-density public spaces (shopping malls, food streets, amusement parks) must not be within residential zones.
6. Public transport stations must be placed within 500m of large public spaces.
7. Roadways must not be built through or around residential zones.
8. Buildings must not be placed within 50m of riverbanks.
9. Green zones must cover at least 10% of each district.
10. Commercial buildings above 5 storeys must be within 400m of an LRT/MRT/BRT station.
11. Highways must not pass through heritage zones.
12. Waste processing facilities must be at least 3km from hospitals and schools.
13. Petrol stations must not be within 50m of residential buildings.
14. Each residential zone must have at least one public park within 1km.
15. Hawker centres must be within 500m of residential zones.
